UMATTER2US is a not for profit organisation based in the UK

We provide emotional support and advice to people who are experiencing emotional stress caused by their family situations.

  • Are you experiencing emotional stress caused by changes to your family situation/arrangement
  • Are you experiencing domestic abuse
  • Are the social services intervening in your family affairs
  • Do you need to talk to somebody about the emotional problems you are experiencing

We are here to help

  • We provide emotional support and advice
  • You can talk to us
  • We would be your friends and advise you or sign post you to the right place for assistance
  • We would not charge you for the assistance that we provide to you
